2 Introduction

2.1 Purpose of this Document

The purpose of this document is to provide the reader with information on the test objectives, methodology, test coverage, schedules, criteria and requirements of the GB18030 conformance test for IBM Cognos Business Intelligence 10.2 on Chinese environment as described below.  This test will be performed by China Software Development Lab (CSDL).

2.2 Scope

The test plan will list all assertions required for testing the IBM Cognos Business Intelligence 10.2.

The objective is to verify the following:

  • Existing functions work correctly for GB18030 conformance in the localized environment.

The test plan will also detail the environment requirements to run the tests.

6 Defect Management

6.1 Defect Identification

Problems with IBM Cognos Business Intelligence 10.2 will be identified based upon analysis of test case results and other observations of the tester. Product operation which contradicts the product documentation and GB18030 compliance will be considered as a defect.

The originator of a defect must provide complete and detailed descriptions of any problem found, as well as instructions on problem reproduction, the build level used when the failure occurred, the type of hardware and software being used, etc.

6.2 Defect Reporting and Tracking

The problems found during the IBM Cognos Business Intelligence 10.2 GB18030 conformance test will be reported as defects using TCT. TCT will be used to report suspected problems, and to track those problems and their resolutions.

All defects must be prefixed by "GB18030" in the abstract. If a defect is determined to be blocking defect, the originator should use the keyword tab, entering both Blocking and GB18030 as keywords for more proficient query.
